# Epistemic Analysis Addendum

Evaluate the claim without assuming either an official or alternative narrative is correct.

Track:

- direct observations;
- original source claims;
- independent corroboration;
- shared source chains;
- relevant counterevidence;
- missing evidence;
- plausible alternatives;
- present conclusion;
- qualitative confidence;
- revision conditions.

Do not convert ambiguity into certainty. Do not use mystery itself as positive evidence for a preferred explanation.
